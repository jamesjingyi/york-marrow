York Marrow x Islamic Society Bone Marrow Recruitment Event (Thurs 23rd Feb)

On Thursday 23rd we are holding a donor recruitment clinic in the JB Morell library foyer from 11am- 5pm where we will collaborate with theUniversity of York Islamic Society. At this clinic, we'll give you more information on what Marrow is all about. If you decide you'd like to be involved, we ask you to fill out a quick form and spit into a tube. You'll then be registered onto the database with all of our amazing other potential lifesavers, so that one day of you're identified as a match, you could save a life.
